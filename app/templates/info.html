All Table Information


This platform allows for the organisation of data required by the NCP funding managment.
This page explains what information is captured in each table.

Table Name Description Connections
Students Contains the each student's personal information for each row. Universities & Campuses
Programs Contains all information regarding the available Programs offered to students. No connections
Universities Contains basic information of every University previously or currently associated with NCP. No connections
Campuses Contains basic information of the different campuses and their respective Universities. Universities
Payments Contains information of all past payments to students. Students & Programs
Grants Contains information of all grants awarded or tentatively awarded to students Programs, Students, Payments, Universities & Campuses
